Frequently Asked Questions

WriteLux is a dynamic and responsive blog platform that allows users to create, share, and explore blog posts with features like user authentication, personalized reading lists, a markdown editor, and the ability to upload header images for posts.

To create an account, click on the "Register" button in the navigation bar, fill out the registration form, and submit it. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

Yes, WriteLux is designed to be fully responsive, providing a seamless experience across all devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Click on the "Login" button in the navigation bar and enter your username and password to log in.

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

After logging in, navigate to your dashboard, where you can update your profile information, including your name, bio, and profile picture.

To create a new post, log in to your account and navigate to the "Create Post" section from the navigation bar. Use the markdown editor to write your post, upload a header image if desired, and click "Publish" when you're ready.

Yes, you can edit or delete your posts. Navigate to your dashboard, select the post you wish to edit or delete, and use the available options to make changes.

In the post creation or editing interface, click on the "Upload Image" button to add images to your post. You can upload multiple images and arrange them as needed.

Navigate to the "Portfolio" section from the navigation bar. Here, you can upload images of your design work and organize them into folders for easy management and presentation.

In the portfolio section, click the "Upload Image" button, select the images you wish to upload, and add descriptions to each image. You can also choose which folder to save them in.

Yes, you can rename or delete folders in your portfolio. Use the checkbox list to select the folders you wish to manage, and click the rename or delete button to perform the action.
